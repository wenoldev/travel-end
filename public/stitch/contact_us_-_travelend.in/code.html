Frequently Asked Questions

Do you customize tour packages?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating personalized itineraries tailored to your interests, budget, and schedule. Just let us know your requirements in the contact form.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance, especially during peak tourist seasons (October to March) to ensure the best availability for hotels and guides.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 7 days prior to the trip are fully refundable. For cancellations within 7 days, a partial fee applies. Please refer to our detailed terms and conditions.
